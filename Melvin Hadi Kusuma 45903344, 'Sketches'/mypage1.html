Trivia

- A drawing usually takes 2-3 hours to draw and colour, a sketch takes about 1 hour.

- Canvas size is usually 1500 * 1500px or 1500 * 1800px.

- I use a brush size of 5px to draw, and then 25px+ to colour in.

- I try not use reference when drawing, otherwise I search up for clothing reference.

- You can observe some progression where I used to only draw from the shoulders/chest up, to drawing more full-body figures and interesting angles. I also started to use less saturated colours for backgrounds, but I may come back to it because they gave off alot of vibrance.

- Digital artists I am influenced by are Ahmed Aldoori and Ilya Kuvshinov.
