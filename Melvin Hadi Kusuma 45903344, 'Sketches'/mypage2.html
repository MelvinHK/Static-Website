About

This site holds drawings that I've created as a result of my loving interest in art as a pastime, as well as the flexibility and possibilites of using a graphics tablet to draw.

I most often draw random female characters in arbitrary poses and clothing. However, with my unpolished drawings, I usually like to draw fiction themes.

______

More About

I've always enjoyed just drawing and sketching what's appeared on my mind when I was a young child. I would frequently show off what I drew to friends and family. But, clumsily, I would always tend to lose or damage the drawings I've made.

It was when I reached my 14th birthday that I recieved my first graphics tablet, and it was absolutely fantastic. One of the many reasons being; I would never lose my drawings, and they would never naturally deteriorate. I could come back to edit or view them while they maintain their crisp resolution because they were drawn digitally.


Some very early drawings I've created, still being able to come back to them and make edits if I want to!


So, with my drawings being able to be stored as digital data, I want to show them off to not only my friends and family, but to all viewers online.
